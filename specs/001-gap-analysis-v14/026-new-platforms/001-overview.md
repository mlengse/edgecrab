# 026 — New Platform Adapters (LINE, SimpleX, Google Chat, MS Teams)

**Tier:** C | **Impact:** 2 | **Value-per-Effort:** 3 | **Risk:** 2
**Primitive moved:** Reach (audience surfaces)

## Why It Matters (First Principles)

Distribution > capability. Hermes v0.14 added four new platform
adapters expanding the gateway's reach to Asian markets (LINE),
privacy users (SimpleX), and enterprise (Google Chat, MS Teams).

## The Gap

EdgeCrab has 17 platform adapters already; missing these 4 leaves
~40% of Asian-Pacific markets unreachable and most enterprises
(Teams) locked out.

## What EdgeCrab Gets Wrong Today

Enterprise pilots inevitably ask "does it work in Teams?" — no.
